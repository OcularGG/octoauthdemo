Available Positions

OCULAR has multiple specialized roles available. Each requires specific skills and training, which we provide to qualified applicants.

Rescue Pilot

Our frontline operators who conduct rescue missions throughout the galaxy. Rescue Pilots navigate to stranded commanders and provide fuel and repairs.

✓ Ship with minimum 35ly jump range
✓ Fuel Scoop and Fuel Transfer Limpets
✓ 100+ hours of flight time
✓ Basic navigation skills

Dispatcher

Dispatchers coordinate rescue operations, matching stranded commanders with the nearest available Rescue Pilots and providing navigation support.

✓ Strong communication skills
✓ Advanced navigation knowledge
✓ Multi-tasking ability
✓ Available for regular shifts

Training Officer

Training Officers mentor new recruits, teaching rescue procedures, fuel management, and emergency protocols to ensure our high standards are maintained.

✓ 1+ year OCULAR experience
✓ 50+ successful rescues
✓ Teaching aptitude
✓ Excellent documentation skills

Recruitment Process

1

Application

Submit your application through our secure portal. You'll need to provide your commander information, experience level, and preferred role.

2

Initial Assessment

Our recruitment team will review your application and conduct an initial assessment of your skills and experience. You'll be contacted for a brief interview.

3

Training Program

Successful applicants enter our comprehensive training program, where you'll learn rescue procedures, navigation techniques, and emergency protocols.

4

Mentored Missions

Once basic training is complete, you'll participate in mentored rescue missions with experienced OCULAR members who will guide and evaluate your performance.

5

Certification

After completing the required number of successful mentored missions, you'll receive your OCULAR certification and be assigned to active duty.
