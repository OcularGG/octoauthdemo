Welcome to OCULAR

We are a volunteer-based organization dedicated to rescuing stranded pilots throughout the galaxy. Our mission is simple: to provide fuel to those who need it, wherever they may be.

24/7 Emergency Response

Our network of dedicated rescue pilots stands ready at all times to assist commanders in distress, no matter where in the galaxy they might be stranded.

Galaxy-Wide Coverage

From the heart of the bubble to the furthest reaches of the galaxy, our rescue teams have the equipment and expertise to reach you.

Free of Charge

All rescue operations are provided at no cost to the stranded commander, funded entirely by donations and our dedicated volunteers.
